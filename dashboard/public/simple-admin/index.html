⛏ Apply to become a validator

You run this node — applying stakes through a staking package: pick one, stake at least the larger of the network minimum and the package minimum (the stake locks for the package term), and the admin will review your application. On approval you receive a unique public APOS code that delegators use to fund your pool.

⏳ Awaiting admin approval

Your validator application is staked and pending. The admin will approve you and issue your public APOS code — this page refreshes automatically.

⚠ Your POOL dropped below the network minimum. Your gas-fee share is paused — you earn nothing while below the minimum. Refill the pool — top up your own stake (Packages tab) or receive new delegations — and once the pool (your stake + delegations) is back above the minimum you are automatically restored to ACTIVE with your tier re-applied.

📦 My Packages — the offers delegators join on your public page

You publish your own delegation packages: a monthly rate (capped by your admin package APR), a lock term, a minimum deposit (no maximum) and an early-exit penalty (max 20%). Delegators join a package on your public page; its terms are locked into their position forever. Disabling a package stops new joins — existing positions keep their terms.

📦 Package rules
One package at a time. Top-ups go into your selected package and re-lock the same term. You can switch packages only after the current term ends. Delegator deposits follow the terms of your packages (My Packages card) — early self-withdraw pays that package's penalty to you.

Application rejected

You can withdraw your self-stake below. Once all delegators have exited, withdrawing fully clears your entry so you can re-apply.

Delegators

Every deposit is its own position inside one of your packages — the package's rate, lock and early-exit penalty are locked in at deposit time (grandfathered). Profit accrues daily; delegators can self-withdraw any time (exiting before the lock ends pays the package's penalty, kept by you), or you can release principal back yourself penalty-free.

Stake into PoS

Stakes are taken from your signed-in validator wallet. The amount must be ≥ the contract's MIN_STAKE. Once staked, Nethermind's PoS sealer will start including you in the proposer round-robin within ~1 block.

Request unstake

Initiates the unstake countdown (100 blocks · ~10 min on testnet). Once the delay has passed the Withdraw button below activates and you get your ELN back. While the request is pending, you stop producing blocks immediately.
