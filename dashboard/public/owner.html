Elyon Chain
Validator & Delegator Network

Delegate to a validator.
Grow the pool. Earn rewards.

Validators run Elyon nodes and are admin-approved with a unique public APOS code. Each validator publishes its own packages — a monthly rate, a lock term, a minimum deposit and an early-exit penalty. Visit a validator's page, pick a package, send a delegation request, chat it out — and once accepted, deposit into that package. Every deposit is its own position that locks in the package's terms forever (grandfathered). Profit accrues daily and you withdraw yourself, any time: principal + accrued profit. Exiting before the package lock ends pays that package's penalty.

Delegate ELN

Enter a validator's public APOS code (or pick one from the cards above), choose one of their packages, and the amount of ELN to deposit. Each deposit creates a new position that locks in the package's rate, lock term and early-exit penalty forever. Deposits count toward the validator's pool (and tier) directly. You can make multiple deposits and withdraw each position yourself (exiting before the lock ends pays the package's penalty).

Rewards are hand-distributed to you by the validator, recorded on-chain — track them under “My delegations”.
